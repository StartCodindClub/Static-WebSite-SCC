Who are we?

With years of experience in the photo and video industry, Pixel Station Productions is recognized as a leading photographer and videographer in the greater Houston area. We strive for a cinematic and story-like quality for all of our productions that preserve your most genuine and precious moments. Our unique team of professionals believes that our photography and videography should capture each of our clients in a way that is unique to them. Founded in 2012, we have quickly established ourselves as a premier studio, delivering high-impact images and video for both individual and business clients. Our years of experience have given us recognition as a leading portrait and wedding photography studio. We are known for our high quality photography and professional video production and are excited and eager to work on any and all unique projects you have. With cinematic vision and available same-day edits, Pixel Station Productions is dedicated to setting a new bar for the industry. We are a client focused company and direct each project with the client’s vision at the forefront. We cannot wait to work with you!
