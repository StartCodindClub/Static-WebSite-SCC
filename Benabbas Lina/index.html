About Us

Get to know us more!

Alpha Living is an algerian interior design business that provides several services:
homes, hotels, offices, restaurants, pools.. We have a total of 19 branches globally: Algeria:Constantine,Algiers,Oran/ Tunisia:Tunis/ Morrocco:Casablanca/ Egypet:Cairo/ UAE:Dubai/ France:Paris,Monaco/ Spain:Madrid/ England:London/ Korea:Seoul/ Japan:Tokyo/ USA:New York,Los Angeles/ Canada:Toronto/ Brazil:Sao Paulo/ India:Mumbai/ South Africa:Cape Town ;an impressive line up right! the company was created in 2012 by Zaid Sitelli: the current owner; Each branch has a director and a team of its own.
The production factories are all located in Algeria delievering the products to the branches by command. Alpha Living never fails its customers and adds a 'if you don't like it we will redo it for free' policy making it the most trusted company by customers: resulting in the 117 projects already done.
