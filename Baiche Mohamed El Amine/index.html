RESEARCH

The college contains scientific research laboratories that lead in post-graduation training, as it provides students with opportunities for training, university qualification and scientific research, which is one of the first laboratories in the field of computer science at the national level. They are: Distributed Computer Science Lab, Complex Systems Modeling and Implementation Lab, Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Lab.

LIBRARY

The college provides the possibilities of exchanging experiences with several foreign universities through student exchange programs. It also provides a library within the university with several privileges. The library contains a reading room with a capacity of 120 places for a student, an internet room with +40 places, a store, an internet service. And many tempting perks. Access to the library is reserved for students, teachers and researchers only

What is the system of study in the college?

The first two years are general computer science, and the specialization starts from the third year and then the first year of the master

What are the majors of Bachelor's and Master's?

There are 4 majors in the bachelor's degree, information systems, software engineering, information technology and computer science. Also in the master there are 4 specializations: networks and distributed systems, information systems and web technology, information and communication sciences and technologies, and software engineering.
