About Us

We believe astronomy is a fundamentally democratic science, with the cosmos equally accessible to all people regardless of age, gender, background, ethnicity, economic status, or abilities. We envision a world where people of all ages and backgrounds engage in astronomy and share their passion for astronomy with others.
